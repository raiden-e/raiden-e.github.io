## Technical Sales & Presales | AI & Cloud

I'm wrapping up my Business Informatics degree at FH Dortmund with a thesis on AI-powered video generation. After 3+ years in internal IT consulting at SITA, I'm moving to **German-speaking Switzerland** for my next chapter: **Technical Sales / Solution Consulting in the DACH region**.

I combine a solid tech background (Python, cloud infrastructure, DevOps) with business acumen and a genuine passion for solving problems with the right software. I only want to sell products I genuinely believe in — technical conviction is the best sales tool, and I'm not interested in the alternative.

**Languages:** German (native), English (fluent)

## Background

I started as a developer and infrastructure engineer (PowerShell, Python, networking), but my Business Informatics studies — especially modules like Consultative Selling during my semester abroad at Western Carolina University — sparked something bigger. I realized I love the human side: understanding pain points, discussing solutions, and making technology work for people, not just systems.

At SITA's Program Office, I've coordinated software rollouts (payment solution migrations across airports in 5+ countries), set up tools (SharePoint, Kimai), and moderated workshops between business, IT, and data teams. I want to be where solutions meet customers — advising, demoing, and closing deals.

My bachelor's thesis dives into `AI-powered video generation from PDFs`, exploring generative AI, multimodal models, avatar synthesis, and ethical implications (deepfakes, consent, authenticity) — a concrete look at how AI transforms content creation and the responsibilities that come with it.
